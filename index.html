About the course

An 8‑week live cohort by GRAPH Courses (University of Geneva) combining workshops, help sessions, a private community, personalized feedback and capstone coaching. Focus: using LLMs and AI tools to automate tasks, analyze data, synthesize literature and communicate research.

1. Websites, Docs & Presentations

LLM basics; Markdown/LaTeX/HTML; research sites; AI presentation tools.

2. AI‑Automated Data Analysis

Code Interpreter, Julius, TheBricks; dashboards & reports.

3. GenAI for Research & Text

Literature reviews (Elicit/LiteRev); classification; sentiment; knowledge maps.

4. Custom GPTs & Chatbots

Chat architecture, context windows, RAG; Botpress; knowledge bases.

5. AI for Task Automation

Email flows, browser agents and multi‑step automations.

6. Communication & Outreach

AI summaries, figures, video abstracts & scheduling.

7. Capstone & Showcase

Progress checks, peer reviews and final presentation.
